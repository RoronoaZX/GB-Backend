## 2. Fixed the Negative Sales Math Bug
*   **The Problem:** The client brilliantly pointed out that if the new Repurposing button instantly deducted stock, the Sales Lady entering her "Bread Out" at the end of the day would cause a double-deduction, resulting in "negative" sales on her report.
*   **The Fix:** Modified the backend (`BreadOutController.php`) to safely remove the instant deduction. The system now perfectly aligns with the manual end-of-day accounting process.

## 3. Discovered the "Morning Delivery Wipeout" Race Condition
*   **The Audit:** Conducted a deep-dive analysis of the entire Sales Report transaction flow (`BranchReportController.php`).
*   **The Vulnerability:** Discovered that the live database completely resets its counters *only when the Administrator clicks Confirm*. Because of the 4:00 AM / 1:00 PM shift timings, if the Administrator clicks Confirm *after* the next shift's Baker delivers fresh bread, the system accidentally deletes the Baker's delivery.
*   **The Plan:** Designed a permanent fix for this called the **Safe Handover Architecture**.

## 4. Designed the "Safe Handover" Architecture
*   **Rollover Shift:** Instead of resetting inventory on the Administrator's "Confirm", mapped out a plan to reset the inventory exactly when the PM Sales Lady clicks **"Submit"** at 4:00 AM. 
*   **Safe Confirm/Decline:** Redesigned the "Confirm" and "Decline" buttons to act as a pure shift handover verification, allowing the AM Cashier to intelligently deduct discrepancies without touching the AM Baker's new bread deliveries.
